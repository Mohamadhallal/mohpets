Animales we sell

types of Dogs
  • Labrador Retriever: Labrador Retrievers are one of the most popular dog breeds known for their friendly and outgoing nature. They are intelligent, loyal, and make excellent family pets.
  • German Shepherd: German Shepherds are well-known for their versatility and intelligence. They are often used as working dogs in various roles, including police, military, search and rescue, and service dogs.
  • Bulldog: Bulldogs are known for their distinctive wrinkled face and loose, saggy skin. They have a calm and friendly demeanor, making them great companions.
  • Siberian Husky: Siberian Huskies are a medium-sized working breed known for their striking appearance and thick double coat. They are energetic, social, and known for their strong sled-pulling abilities.
types of Cats
  • Siamese: Siamese cats are a popular and distinctive breed known for their striking blue almond-shaped eyes and short, sleek coat with color points (darker fur on their ears, face, paws, and tail). They are social, affectionate, and enjoy interacting with their human companions.
  • Maine Coon: Maine Coons are one of the largest domestic cat breeds and are known for their long, luxurious fur, bushy tails, and tufted ears. They are friendly, gentle, and often described as "gentle giants." Maine Coons are also highly adaptable and get along well with children and other pets.
  • Scottish Fold: Scottish Folds are recognized for their unique folded ears that give them an endearing and owl-like appearance. They have a sweet, calm temperament and are generally affectionate towards their owners. Their unique physical feature is a result of a genetic mutation.
types of Rabbits
  • Mini Lop: Mini Lops are a small and adorable breed known for their lop ears and compact size. They are friendly, affectionate, and have gentle personalities, making them great companions for families and individuals. Mini Lops are relatively easy to handle and can adapt well to indoor living.
